Recipe for Avocado

The avocado is a fruit that is native to south-central Mexico, classified as a member of the flowering plant family Lauraceae.

It is known for its creamy texture and mild flavor. Avocados are often used in culinary applications, particularly in salads, spreads (such as guacamole), and sandwiches. They are also a good source of healthy monounsaturated fats, vitamins, and minerals.
