Recipe for Pilk

Pilk is a portmaneteau of Pepsi and milk, it is creamy and sweet and apparently pairs perfectly with cookies. This combines the beverages Pepsi and milk together, and it is touted as an incredibly tasty mixture online.

How-to create: Pilk


  1. Pour a can of Pepsi into a chilled glass

  2. Top off the drink with some full-fat milk

  3. Enjoy! (Or mix first)
